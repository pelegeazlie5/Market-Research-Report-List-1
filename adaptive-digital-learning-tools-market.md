<p><p>Adaptive digital learning tools are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based tools are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access for users. They often facilitate real-time updates and collaborative features. Each type serves distinct institutional needs, balancing accessibility with management preferences and regulatory requirements.</p></p>

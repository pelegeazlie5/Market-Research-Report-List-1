<p><p>The Automated Test Equipment (ATE) market comprises several key segments. Memory ATE, on the other hand, specializes in testing memory chips and modules, validating their speed, capacity, and reliability. Each segment caters to distinct testing requirements within the electronics manufacturing industry.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Monofilament</li><li>Multifilament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The collagen fiber suture market is primarily categorized into two types: monofilament and multifilament sutures. Monofilament sutures consist of a single strand of collagen, offering smooth passage through tissue and reducing the risk of infection; they are ideal for delicate surgeries. Conversely, multifilament sutures are made of multiple strands twisted together, providing enhanced strength and flexibility, making them suitable for larger tissue repairs. Both types serve diverse surgical applications, catering to varying clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>

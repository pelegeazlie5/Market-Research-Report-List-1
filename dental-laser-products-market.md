<p><p>The dental laser products market is segmented into various types based on the technology used. Diode lasers, known for their versatility and efficiency, are commonly used in soft tissue procedures. Er:YAG lasers (2940nm) are effective for hard tissue applications, offering precise cutting and minimal thermal damage. Nd:YAG lasers (1064nm) excel in both soft and hard tissue surgeries while also providing effective periodontal treatments. Other laser types include CO2 and fiber lasers, catering to specific dental applications and expanding treatment possibilities.</p></p>

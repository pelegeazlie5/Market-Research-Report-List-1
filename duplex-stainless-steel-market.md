<p><p>The duplex stainless steel market is categorized into three main types: lean duplex, duplex, and super duplex. Lean duplex possesses lower alloy content, offering a cost-effective solution with good corrosion resistance and strength. Duplex stainless steel features a balanced composition, providing enhanced performance in various environments. Super duplex contains higher levels of chromium and molybdenum, delivering superior corrosion resistance and mechanical properties, making it ideal for demanding applications such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and marine industries.</p></p>

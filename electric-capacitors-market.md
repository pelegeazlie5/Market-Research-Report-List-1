<p><p>The electric capacitors market comprises various types catering to diverse applications. Ceramic capacitors are known for their stability and reliability, ideal for high-frequency circuits. Film/paper capacitors offer excellent dielectric properties and are suitable for audio applications. Aluminium capacitors are widely used for power supply and energy storage due to their high capacitance values. Tantalum/niobium capacitors provide compact size and reliability for specialized electronic devices. Double-layer/super capacitors are designed for rapid charge and discharge, making them ideal for energy storage systems.</p></p>

<p><p>The EVA adhesive film market features two primary types: transparent EVA film and white EVA film. Transparent EVA film is widely used for applications requiring visibility and clarity, such as laminating and packaging, offering strong adhesion while maintaining aesthetic appeal. White EVA film, on the other hand, is favored for its opacity and is commonly employed in industries like automotive and construction, where hiding underlying materials is essential. Both types provide excellent performance in bonding and sealing applications across various sectors.</p></p>

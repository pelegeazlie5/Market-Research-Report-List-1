<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IC Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FC-BGA</li><li>FC-CSP</li><li>WB BGA</li><li>WB CSP</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC substrates are crucial in semiconductor packaging, facilitating electrical connections and mechanical support. The market can be segmented into several types: </p><p>1. **FC-BGA (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array)** uses solder balls for connections, enabling high-density interconnections.</p><p>2. **FC-CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package)** is a compact design allowing for space-efficient applications.</p><p>3. **WB BGA (Wire Bond Ball Grid Array)** connects chips using wire bonds, suitable for larger packages.</p><p>4. **WB CSP (Wire Bond Chip Scale Package)** offers a smaller footprint similar to FC-CSP but uses wire bonding.</p><p>5. **Others** encompass various alternative substrates and specialized designs tailored for specific applications.</p></p>

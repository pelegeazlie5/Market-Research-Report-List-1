<p><p>The industrial metal cleaners market primarily comprises two types: alkaline cleaners and acid cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are formulated with high pH levels, effective for removing oils, greases, and other organic contaminants from metallic surfaces, making them ideal for processes like degreasing. Acid cleaners, on the other hand, utilize low pH formulations to effectively eliminate rust, scale, and mineral deposits. Each type is tailored for specific applications, ensuring efficient surface preparation for various industrial processes and enhancing metal surface integrity.</p></p>

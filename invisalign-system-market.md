<p><p>The Invisalign System market is primarily classified into two types: Mouth Invisalign System and Skeleton Invisalign System. The Mouth Invisalign System focuses on transparent aligners that fit snugly over teeth, providing effective teeth straightening while being virtually invisible. On the other hand, the Skeleton Invisalign System emphasizes a more rigid framework that supports teeth movement through a combination of aligners and additional structures, offering enhanced control over complex orthodontic cases. Both systems cater to diverse orthodontic needs and patient preferences.</p></p>

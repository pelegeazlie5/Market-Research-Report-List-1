<p><p>The Laser Cleaning Head market is primarily divided into Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed laser types. Continuous Wave lasers emit a constant beam of light, providing a steady cleaning action suitable for larger surfaces and more uniform contamination removal. In contrast, Pulsed lasers produce short bursts of energy, allowing for precise cleaning of intricate or sensitive areas, minimizing heat impact. Both types offer unique advantages, catering to diverse applications across industries such as automotive, aerospace, and conservation.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Continuous Compression</li><li>Intermittent Compression</li><li>Sequential Compression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leg compression machine market encompasses three main types: continuous compression, intermittent compression, and sequential compression. Continuous compression provides a steady pressure to enhance blood circulation and reduce swelling. Intermittent compression alternates between applying and releasing pressure, promoting dynamic blood flow and comfort. Sequential compression uses a wave-like motion, targeting different segments of the leg in a specific order to maximize circulation and minimize the risk of deep vein thrombosis. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to various medical needs.</p></p>

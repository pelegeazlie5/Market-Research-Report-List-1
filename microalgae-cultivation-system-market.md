<p><p>The microalgae cultivation system market is divided into two main types: closed training and open training systems. Closed training systems involve controlled environments, such as photobioreactors, that optimize growth conditions, reduce contamination risks, and enhance productivity. In contrast, open training systems utilize natural bodies of water, like ponds, allowing for easier scalability and lower operational costs but with higher risks of contamination and environmental fluctuations. Each system offers distinct advantages depending on the intended application and production scale.</p></p>

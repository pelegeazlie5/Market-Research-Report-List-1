<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Machines</li><li>Mechanical Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mineral flotation machines market is primarily divided into pneumatic and mechanical machines. Pneumatic machines utilize Air or gas to create bubbles for mineral separation, offering efficient processing with minimal energy consumption. In contrast, mechanical machines rely on agitators and mixers to enhance bubble formation and mineral attachment. Their design promotes greater contact between minerals and air, improving recovery rates. Both types cater to diverse operational needs, influencing their adoption across mining and mineral processing industries globally.</p></p>

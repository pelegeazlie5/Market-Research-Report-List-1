<p><p>The Motorcycle Brake by Wire (BBW) System operates using electronic controls to manage braking forces, enhancing performance and safety. The market is categorized into single-channel and multiple-channel systems. Single-channel systems utilize one channel to control brakes, typically found in basic models, offering cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multiple-channel systems feature several channels for independent control of individual brakes, delivering improved handling, stability, and responsiveness, making them suitable for high-performance motorcycles that demand advanced braking capabilities.</p></p>

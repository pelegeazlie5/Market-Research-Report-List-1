<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Devices Market encompasses various methodologies to evaluate materials without causing damage. X-Ray testing uses radiation to inspect internal structures, while Ultrasonic testing employs high-frequency sound waves for precision analysis. Magnetic Particle testing detects surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials, and Penetrant Flaw Detection reveals surface-breaking defects through dye penetration. Eddy Current Testing identifies defects in conductive materials using electromagnetic induction. Other methods may include visual inspection and thermography, expanding the scope of NDT applications across industries.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Optical Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSD Method</li><li>CMOS (CCD) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical displacement sensors market is primarily categorized into two main types: PSD (Position Sensitive Device) Method and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor, including CCD - Charge-Coupled Device) Method. The PSD Method utilizes a position-sensitive detector to convert displacement into an electrical signal, ideal for precision measurement. In contrast, the CMOS method leverages semiconductor technology for capturing images and detecting displacements, offering high-speed performance and low power consumption. Both methods are widely used in industrial automation, robotics, and precision engineering applications.</p></p>

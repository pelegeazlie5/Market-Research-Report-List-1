<p><ul><li>Single-girder Overhead Traveling Cranes</li><li>Double-girder Overhead Traveling Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overhead traveling cranes market is primarily categorized into single-girder and double-girder types. Single-girder overhead traveling cranes feature a single main beam, making them lightweight and suitable for lighter loads and lower ceilings. They are cost-effective and easy to maintain. In contrast, double-girder overhead traveling cranes consist of two main beams, allowing them to handle heavier loads and providing increased stability and height clearance. These cranes are ideal for industrial applications requiring greater lifting capacity and versatility.</p></p>

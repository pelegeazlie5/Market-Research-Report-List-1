<p><p>The particle size distribution measurement instruments market includes various technologies for analyzing particle sizes in different applications. Laser diffraction measures particle size by analyzing light patterns caused by particles passing through a laser beam, allowing rapid and broad size distribution assessments. Dynamic light scattering determines size by measuring the Brownian motion of particles in suspension. Nanoparticle tracking analysis visualizes and tracks individual nanoparticles to provide real-time size distribution data. Other methods may include sieve analysis and microscopy techniques, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>

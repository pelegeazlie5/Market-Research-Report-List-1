PLGA 50:50 offers balanced properties but faster degradation, suitable for short-term applications. PLGA 65:35 and PLGA 75:25 provide moderate degradation with enhanced strength, ideal for sustained release systems. PLGA 85:15 demonstrates slow degradation, making it suitable for long-term applications and controlled drug release formulations.</p></p>

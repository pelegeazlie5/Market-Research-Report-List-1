<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU</li><li>POE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential water treatment equipment market is primarily categorized into Point-of-Use (POU) and Point-of-Entry (POE) systems. POU systems treat water at the point where it is consumed, such as under-sink filters or faucets, ensuring immediate access to purified water. In contrast, POE systems treat water at the main entry point of the home, providing whole-house filtration and protection against contaminants for all water used in the household. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs for clean and safe water.</p></p>

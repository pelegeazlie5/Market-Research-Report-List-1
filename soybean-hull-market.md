<p><p>The soybean hull market consists of two primary types: loose form and pellet form. Loose soybean hulls are unprocessed and typically used in livestock feed, providing fiber and bulk. In contrast, pelletized soybean hulls are compressed into uniform pellets, offering convenience in storage and handling while retaining nutritional value. The pellet form is often favored for consistency in feeding and reduced dust, making it suitable for commercial feed operations. Both forms serve vital roles in livestock nutrition and overall agricultural production.</p></p>

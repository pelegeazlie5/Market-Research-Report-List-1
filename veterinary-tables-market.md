<p><p>Veterinary tables are essential tools in animal healthcare, categorized into four market types based on their operating mechanisms. Electric tables offer adjustable height and positioning for ease of use during procedures. Hydraulic tables provide adjustable settings through hydraulic systems, facilitating stability and support. Manual tables rely on manual adjustment, often being cost-effective and straightforward. Pneumatic tables utilize air pressure for height adjustment, offering smooth transitions and flexibility, catering to various veterinary needs across clinics and hospitals.</p></p>

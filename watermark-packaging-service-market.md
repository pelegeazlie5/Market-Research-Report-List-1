<p><p>The Watermark Packaging Service market is divided into two main types: Visible Watermark and Invisible Watermark. Visible Watermarks are easily discernible marks embedded in packaging to deter counterfeiting and enhance brand recognition. These are often used on labels and cartons. In contrast, Invisible Watermarks are undetectable to the naked eye, allowing for discreet tracking and authentication of products. These technologies cater to different security needs, with visible watermarks promoting brand identity and invisible watermarks ensuring product integrity throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
